HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to Sharks and Minnows! You are are a minnow, and are represented by a green dot. The goal of the game is to get to the top of the pool without getting touched by any sharks. Sharks are represented by bigger red dots. The longer you survive, the more sharks there will be!
